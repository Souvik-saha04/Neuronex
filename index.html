🏥 About the Project

Neuronex is a unified digital health platform built for India's migrant worker population — ensuring seamless, secure, and multilingual access to healthcare records, disease tracking, and government health interventions across state boundaries.

Millions of migrant workers lose continuity of care every year when they move between states. Neuronex solves this through a portable digital health ID, AI-driven outbreak detection, OCR-powered prescription intake, and a three-tier role-based access system connecting workers, hospitals, and government bodies.

🔐 Role-Based Access System

The platform operates a two-tier role hierarchy with encrypted data and granular permission controls:

RoleAccess LevelCapabilities
Admin Hospital / District / Government View all user data, upload & scan prescriptions via OCR, verify documents, edit records, manage Worker Registry and Medical Records Store, generate outbreak reports, receive AI-generated predictive alerts.
User Individual Worker Manage own health records, view prescriptions & lab results, link accounts as a family group, receive health alerts, access offline with Aadhaar-based digital ID.

✨ Key Features

For Workers

  • Portable Digital Health ID — Aadhaar-linked, valid across all states
  • Offline Support — Works without internet, syncs later
  • Multilingual UI — Malayalam, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, English
  • Group Accounts — Families share access securely
  • Full Transparency — View prescriptions, vaccinations, lab results

For Government & Hospitals

  • Real-time Outbreak Alerts — AI detects infectious disease hotspots
  • OCR Prescription Intake — Scan printed prescriptions automatically
  • Disease Tracking Dashboards — Heatmaps and real-time reporting
  • Bidirectional Health Protection — Notifies home-state departments
  • Blood Analysis AI — Glucose tracking, cardiac risk detection

AI & Data Layer

  • Vector DB (Pinecone) — Feeds AI agent for predictive responses
  • LangChain AI Agent — Generates do's & don'ts alerts for users
  • Predictive Disease Detection — Early warning for community spread
  • Smart Vaccination Tracking — Ensures no coverage gaps

Uniqueness

  • Home-State Notification — Worker's origin state gets alerted on return
  • QR-Based Record Retrieval — Instant access at any health facility
  • API Integration — Connects with existing state e-health platforms
  • Free for Migrants — Funded via NGOs, CSR & government programs

📊 Feasibility & Viability

Why It Works

  • Lightweight frameworks with offline-first local storage
  • Open-source tooling keeps MVP cost extremely low
  • API integration with existing state e-health platforms (e.g. Kerala's)
  • Scalable via government agencies and NGO backing

Challenges Addressed

  • Duplicate profiles prevented via Aadhaar + biometric validation
  • Integration with partial e-health systems via API bridges
  • Free for migrants — monetized via institutional partnerships
  • Data encryption at all three access levels

🌍 Impact & Benefits

For Migrant Workers

  • Health records accessible anytime, anywhere
  • Continuity of care across state & job changes
  • Transparency into prescriptions & vaccination history
  • Empowerment over personal health data

For Public Health System

  • Early disease outbreak detection via AI heatmaps
  • Vaccination gap tracking — TB, measles, polio
  • Data-driven health policy decisions
  • Supports UN SDGs: health equity & reduced inequalities
